# The Best Time to Take Vitamin D

Vitamin D is crucial for bone health and immune function. However, many people take it at the wrong time, reducing its effectiveness.

## Take it with Meals

Vitamin D is a fat-soluble vitamin. This means it doesn't dissolve in water and is absorbed best in your bloodstream when paired with high-fat foods. 

Taking it alongside a meal that contains healthy fats (like avocados, nuts, or olive oil) can significantly increase absorption.

## Morning or Evening?

There's evidence suggesting that taking Vitamin D late at night might interfere with sleep for some individuals by suppressing melatonin production. For optimal results, aim for **breakfast or lunch**.
